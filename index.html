❓ How to Play

🎯 The goal

Conquer every enemy city on the map. Cities recruit troops over time (up to a cap). Neutral gray cities don't grow — they're the easiest early conquests. When the enemy owns no cities and has no armies marching, you win.

🖱 Controls & the chain attack

Drag from one of your cities to any city and release — half your troops march there. The chain: press on city A, sweep through B, C, D… (all yours), release on the target: every linked city attacks at once. This is how you crack big garrisons. Use the ×-button in battle (or the menu setting) to slow the game to 0.5× or speed it to 2×.

⭐ Stars & par time

Win fast for 3 stars, moderately fast for 2, any win earns 1. Stars unlock generals (up to 33★ for the legends). Replay old battles to improve your stars anytime.

✦ Merit & the War Council

Every battle pays merit — wins pay big (first win of the day is 2×, streaks add bonuses), and even defeats salvage +10. Spend merit at the 🏛 War Council on permanent upgrades: recruit speed, march speed, attack, and starting troops. If you're stuck, grind 2–3 battles, buy an upgrade, and try again. Your leaderboard rank uses lifetime merit and never drops when you spend.

👑 Generals, XP & duo bonds

Each kingdom fields six generals with different passives. Your commanding general earns XP every battle and their perk grows +5% per level (max Lv10). Unlock certain pairs and their duo bond adds a second bonus — see the Generals screen.

👹 Bosses & difficulty tiers

Every 10th battle is a boss with a crowned city and a special ability (announced in the briefing — plan around it!). Difficulty runs on 10 tiers from Recruit to Legend, ramping smoothly across the 200-battle campaign. Watch the tier chip at the top of every battle.

🗺 Weekly Challenge & badges

One shared map per week — everyone races the same battlefield for the fastest clear (top times on the Leaderboard screen). Badges and crowns are earned for campaign milestones, streaks, and feats; show them off on your profile.

🔒 Protecting your commander

Set a passcode on the login screen and nobody else can log into your name. Forgot it? The game admin can clear it from the separate Admin Console (admin.html).
